# The Veridian Manifesto: A Path to 100% Green Energy

Our city is more than just streets and buildings; it is a living ecosystem. The Veridian Shift is our commitment to a future where every kilowatt consumed is a kilowatt harvested from renewable sources. 

EcoPulse is the Digital Twin of this transformation. Through transparency and citizen initiative, we will:
1. Decentralize our energy grid via solar and wind nodes.
2. Incentivize sustainable building practices.
3. Pulse with real-time health data, ensuring no neighborhood is left in the dark.
